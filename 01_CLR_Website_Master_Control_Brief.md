# Central Lakes Removals Website Master Control Brief

## 1. Purpose of this document

This document defines the strategic control system for the new Central Lakes Removals website.

Use it to keep the website clear, premium, owner led, human first, and consistent across design, copy, SEO, structure, and development.

It is written for coding agents, design agents, Figma agents, SEO agents, copy agents, and anyone building or modifying the website.

## 2. Core commercial position

Central Lakes Removals is not to be presented as a generic moving company.

The correct position is:

> Central Lakes Removals is a premium, owner led moving company based in Cromwell, personally led by Russell Brown, built for customers who want their move handled carefully, professionally, and with real accountability.

Russell Brown is the core commercial differentiator.

The company is the delivery system.

The website must make that distinction obvious.

## 3. Primary visitor impression

The visitor should quickly understand:

1. This is personally led by the owner.
2. This is not a faceless removals company.
3. This is for people who want the job done properly.
4. The service is calm, careful, experienced, and accountable.
5. The company is based in Cromwell and serves Central Otago and wider South Island routes.
6. The quote process should feel considered, not generic.

## 4. Brand positioning language

Use this language often:

1. Owner led
2. Personally led by Russell Brown
3. Carefully planned
4. Professionally managed
5. Direct accountability
6. Experienced judgement
7. Higher standard of care
8. Based in Cromwell
9. Serving Central Otago and wider South Island routes
10. Properly planned quote

Avoid this language unless specifically justified:

1. Cheap
2. Budget
3. Discount
4. Stress free
5. Best price
6. Cheapest movers
7. Fast and easy
8. We do it all
9. No worries mate
10. Generic claims without proof

## 5. Proof rules

The website may use these claims only if accurate at time of publishing:

1. 12,000 plus relocations
2. 5.0 Google rating
3. Family owned
4. Based in Cromwell
5. Personally led by Russell Brown
6. One dedicated team

Do not invent review content.

Do not create fake customer names.

Do not claim physical locations in towns where the business is not physically based.

Correct location language:

> Based in Cromwell. Serving Central Otago and wider South Island routes.

Incorrect location language:

> Based in Queenstown, Wanaka, Christchurch, Dunedin, and Invercargill.

## 6. Strategic website goals

The website must do four jobs:

1. Convert serious moving enquiries.
2. Establish Russell Brown as the trust anchor.
3. Strengthen local SEO and service area relevance.
4. Differentiate Central Lakes Removals from volume based movers and generic removal companies.

## 10. Design direction

The visual direction must be:

1. Dark midnight blue or near black background
2. Generous negative space
3. Warm off white typography
4. Muted grey supporting text
5. Brassy gold CTAs
6. Premium editorial typography
7. Real imagery of Russell
8. Minimal clutter
9. Calm, controlled, confident design rhythm

The site should feel closer to:

1. A high end architectural practice
2. A private client service firm
3. A specialist owner operator portfolio
4. A premium local expert brand

The site must not feel like:

1. A generic moving company site
2. A cheap tradie site
3. A loud franchise site
4. A discount driven landing page
5. A stock photo template

## 12. CTA system

Primary CTA:

> Request a Quote

Secondary CTA:

> Talk to Russell

Supporting CTA options:

1. Start Planning Your Move
2. Get a Properly Planned Quote
3. View Moving Services
4. Read Customer Reviews
5. Meet Russell
6. See Service Areas

CTA rules:

1. Use brass mainly for primary conversion actions.
2. Use secondary ghost buttons for calls or supporting actions.
3. Do not use too many competing CTAs in one section.
4. Keep the main conversion path obvious.
5. Do not make the design feel desperate or sales heavy.

## 16. Page template strategy

### Home

Job: explain the premium owner led positioning and convert visitors.

### About Russell

Job: make Russell the human trust anchor and explain owner led accountability.

### Moving Services

Job: show the main service categories and route visitors to specific pages.

### House Moving

Job: rank and convert for household moving enquiries.

### Furniture Moving

Job: rank and convert for smaller furniture moves and high care handling.

### Long Distance Moving

Job: rank and convert for regional and intercity moving.

### Packing and Materials

Job: support upsell and reduce move day risk.

### Service Area Pages

Job: build local relevance while staying truthful about Cromwell as the base.

### Reviews

Job: make customer proof easy to scan and trust.

### Contact or Request a Quote

Job: make enquiry simple, calm, and complete.

## 17. Anti drift rules

All agents must obey these rules:

1. Do not make the site look like a generic removals template.
2. Do not remove Russell from the centre of the positioning.
3. Do not make the site light, bright, cartoonish, or discount driven.
4. Do not overuse brass.
5. Do not hide the Cromwell base.
6. Do not invent claims, reviews, or service locations.
7. Do not write vague filler copy.
8. Do not use stock looking hero imagery.
9. Do not make every section a card grid.
10. Do not prioritise decoration over trust and conversion.
